# Digital Marketing Project Report: OurERP

This repository contains the comprehensive 30-page project report for the digital marketing strategy I executed for **OurERP** (`ourerp.ourerp.org`).


##  Project Overview

A detailed breakdown of the multi-channel digital marketing project for OurERP, a B2B ERP solutions provider.

* **Website:** `https://ourerp.ourerp.org/`
* **My Role:** Digital Marketing Executive
* **Project Duration:** April 2025 – November 2025
* **Project Focus:** To build a digital footprint from the ground up, establish brand visibility, and generate qualified leads through a combination of SEO, paid media, content management, and analytics.

##  Key Objectives

The primary goals for this project were to:

* Improve website visibility and establish initial SEO rankings.
* Drive targeted traffic through strategic Google and Meta ad campaigns.
* Enhance social media presence and community engagement.
* Design compelling creatives and ad banners aligned with brand identity.
* Deliver measurable growth in impressions, clicks, and conversions.

---

## Core Strategies & Execution

I managed the full execution of the digital marketing plan, from initial strategy to final analysis.

### 1. SEO & Content Management
* **Content:** Drafted and optimized all landing pages, service pages, and blog posts, ensuring alignment with SEO keywords and brand voice.
* **On-Page SEO:** Conducted keyword research and competitor analysis to optimize all on-page elements (Meta titles, descriptions, H1/H2 tags, image alts).
* **Off-Page SEO:** Implemented a foundational backlink strategy through high-quality directory submissions.
* **CMS Management:** Built and managed all pages using **WordPress**, installing and configuring necessary plugins for performance and SEO.

### 2. Paid Advertising (Meta Ads)
* **Platform:** Managed campaigns exclusively on **Meta (Facebook/Instagram)**, as no Google Ads budget was allocated for this project.
* **Strategy:** Developed targeted audience segments and custom ad creatives.
* **Optimization:** Actively managed campaign budgets and bid strategies to maximize ROI and achieve a low cost-per-click (CPC).

### 3. Social Media Management
* **Platforms:** Managed **Instagram** and **Facebook**.
* **Execution:** Created a monthly content calendar, designed all post/story creatives, and handled all community engagement (replies, comments, DMs).

### 4. Analytics & Reporting
* **GA4:** Monitored and analyzed user acquisition, engagement, and audience behavior.
* **GSC:** Used Google Search Console to track indexing status, keyword performance (impressions/clicks), and technical SEO health.

---

##  Key Achievements & Results

This strategy successfully took OurERP from a low-visibility state to a platform with a steady stream of traffic and leads.

### 1. Paid Advertising (Meta)
* **Impressions:** 11,300+
* **Website Clicks:** 2,500+
* **Average CPC:** **₹4.74** (Highly cost-efficient)
* **Conversions:** 7 (Direct Website Application Submissions)

### 2. SEO & Organic Growth
* **Organic Impressions:** 2,324
* **Organic Clicks:** 8 (Building from a zero-click baseline)
* **Technical SEO:** Successfully indexed all key landing pages and service pages.
* **User Base:** Attracted **108 Active Users** via organic and paid channels.
* **Audience Quality:** Maintained a **25% Engagement Rate**, indicating high-quality, relevant traffic.
